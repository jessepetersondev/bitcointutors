Lesson 21: Your Bitcoin Journey

Congratulations on completing all the lessons! What's next?

Congratulations! You've completed all 21 Bitcoin Newbie lessons! You now know more about Bitcoin than most adults. This is just the beginning of your Bitcoin journey - there's always more to learn and explore!

Key Points:

  • You've learned the basics of Bitcoin and how it works
  • Learning about money is a lifelong journey
  • There are many ways to continue learning about Bitcoin
  • Always be curious and keep asking questions!

Let's review what you've learned in these lessons:

  • What Bitcoin is and how it works
  • How the blockchain keeps track of all Bitcoin transactions
  • How Bitcoin wallets work and how to keep them safe
  • Why Bitcoin is valuable and how it's different from regular money
  • How Bitcoin gives people more control over their own money
  • How Bitcoin can help protect your savings over time

If you want to continue learning about Bitcoin, here are some things you can do:

  • Talk to your parents about what you've learned and ask if they'd like to learn more with you
  • Read books about Bitcoin that are written for young people
  • Watch educational videos about Bitcoin (with your parents' permission)
  • Try some Bitcoin games or apps that make learning fun
  • Start a small savings plan with your parents' help - maybe part of your allowance could be saved in Bitcoin

Remember: Bitcoin is still new and growing. By learning about it now, you're getting a head start on understanding something that might be very important in your future!

Thank you for joining us on this Bitcoin adventure. Keep being curious, keep asking questions, and keep learning about how money works!
